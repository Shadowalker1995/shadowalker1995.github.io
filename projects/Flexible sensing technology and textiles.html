Flexible sensing technology and textiles

Flexible sensing technology is a very challenging and potential development direction, and with the rapid development of human-computer interaction and sports health monitoring, its application will be very extensive. This study is mainly to use traditional textile technology to prepare sensing yarn, design fabric structure and weaving method and weave intelligent fabrics with sensing function, which can be used in intelligent textile products, flexible electronic products, electronic skin and other fields.
