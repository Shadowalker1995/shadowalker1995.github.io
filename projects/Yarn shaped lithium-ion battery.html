Yarn shaped lithium-ion battery

Lithium-ion batteries are one of the most promising secondary batteries currently in development. However, conventional lithium-ion batteries have many problems such as non-deformability, flammability, and leakage, so they cannot be used as energy storage devices for wearable devices. In this direction, the preparation of a yarn shaped lithium ion battery with woven properties is mainly studied. Moreover, it can be woven into a garment for further application.
