Fiber shaped supercapacitors

Supercapacitors, also known as electrochemical capacitors, stand out among energy storage devices because of their high power density, fast charging and discharging rates, good cycling performance and environmental friendliness. On this basis, the fiber shaped supercapacitor also has the characteristics of light weight, soft, knittable and wearable, but its energy density is low. In this direction, we mainly study the preparation and characterization of high performance fiber shaped supercapacitors, and then weave them into traditional textiles to further promote their applications in wearable electronic devices.
